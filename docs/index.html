***
1. Introduce
This is a game called OMOK in Korea.
It have be played by two people.
The play who place five stones in a line under the rules will be a winner.
***

2. Tutorial
(1). Open the file where you saved OMOK
(2). Type "./omok" for executing
(3). Play with your friend
(4). For Reset or Exit, type proper key
(5). Enjoy. ***
